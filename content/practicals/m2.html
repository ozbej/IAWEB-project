HCI Practical Exercises

(Human-Computer Interaction 3VU SS 2022)

Second Meeting with Client (M2)

After performing your heuristic evaluation, you will have a meeting (Abgabegespräch) with your client (your tutor) at the second client meeting (M2).

The dates and times for meeting M2 are given in the M2 Slots.

You should:

  • Give a 20-minute presentation of the results of your heuristic evaluation to your client.

Bear in mind:

  • All members of your group must attend the client meetings (unless you have a very good reason backed up by documentary evidence such as a doctor's letter).

  • Bring your own laptop.

    If you do not have access to a laptop, contact your tutor well in advance to make appropriate arrangements for a laptop to be available.

  • A projector will be available to project your presentation. In most rooms, the projector will have both VGA and HDMI connectors. You must bring any adapters which are necessary for your laptop.

  • Start up your laptop and load up your slides before the meeting while you are outside, so you are ready to start your presentation straight away.
