Grading Review for HCI SS 2022

The grading review will be face-to-face and will take place under whatever COVID regulations are in force at the time.

The university has lifted all COVID restrictions as of 25 Apr 2022. If COVID restrictions are reintroduced, the consolidated time slot shown below will be readjusted accordingly (bookable individual slots per group).

Grading Review

Attendance at the grading review is not compulsory. If you are happy with your grade, you do not have to attend.

If your grade is not what you expected, or if you have any questions about the grading, the grading review is your chance. Your tutor will explain the points which lead to your grade.

We do sometimes make mistakes, for example when entering data or selecting ratings in Sapphire. If we find such a mistake, the points and grade will of course be corrected.
