Slots for MC1

See the general description of the MC Test.

The test will be face-to-face and will take place under whatever COVID regulations are in force at the time.

The university has lifted all COVID restrictions as of 25 Apr 2022. If COVID restrictions are reintroduced, the time slots shown below will be readjusted accordingly (fewer slots with more time in between).

1 First MC Test (MC1)

I expect everyone to attend MC1. You must come to your assigned slot, as indicated below.

You do not have to register separately for MC1 in TUGRAZonline. You do have to notify me if you cannot make it.

If you have a very good reason (backed up by documentary evidence, such as a doctor's letter) why you cannot attend MC1, you may contact me by email (kandrews@iicm.edu) to arrange to attend the extra test (MC1x). Scroll down to the bottom of the page for details.

Regardless of whether or not you attend MC1, you still have the option to attend MC2.

Tue 14 Jun 2022, HS i13, Inffeldg. 16b, basement (MC1)
